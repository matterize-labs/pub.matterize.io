What We Offer

End-to-End IoT Solutions Built on Matter

Full-Stack IoT Development with Matter at the Core

At Matterize Labs, we help smart home companies bring their ideas to life with embedded software development, Matter protocol integration, and certification support. Whether you’re starting from scratch or updating existing hardware, our team delivers reliable, scalable, and Matter-compliant solutions.

Embedded Software Development

Smart Firmware. Built for Performance. We create custom firmware solutions that power connected devices. From resource-constrained microcontrollers to powerful Linux-based systems, we build software optimized for performance, security, and real-world deployment. We specialize in: Embedded Linux (Yocto, Buildroot, OpenWRT) FreeRTOS Zephyr RTOS Our approach ensures low power consumption, high reliability, and long-term maintainability—so your product is always ready for the future.

Matter Firmware Integration

Certified Connectivity. Seamless User Experience. Matter is the new standard for smart home interoperability. We integrate Matter protocol into your device firmware to ensure your product works effortlessly with Apple Home, Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and other major ecosystems. With Matter built-in: Your devices pair faster Work with more ecosystems Stay up to date and secure We don’t just make your products compatible. We make them Matter-ready from day one.
